Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I link my UPI ID to the wallet?

To link your UPI ID, go to the "Bank / UPI Linking" section in the settings, enter your UPI ID, and click "Link UPI".

What should I do if I forgot my password?

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to reset your password via email.

How do I set a monthly budget?

Go to the "Budget & Savings" page, enter the categories for your budget, and set the amount. The wallet will notify you if you exceed your budget.
